Software Developer

Software Full Time

We are looking for a skilled Software Developer to join our core product team. You will be responsible for building robust, scalable applications, from our corporate front doors to the headless e-commerce architectures powering our fashion sub-brands.

Requirements & Responsibilities:
  • Strong proficiency in modern JavaScript/TypeScript and React/Next.js frameworks.
  • Experience building and maintaining Node.js backends and RESTful APIs.
  • Familiarity with headless CMS integrations and modern cloud infrastructure (AWS/Vercel).
  • Ability to troubleshoot complex integrations and optimize web performance.

AI Automation Intern

AI Internship

Join our experimental AI lab to build workflows and prototypes using the latest LLMs. This is a hands-on role where you will learn to orchestrate AI agents for real business use cases, bridging the gap between automation and our daily operations.

What you'll learn & do:
  • Master advanced prompt engineering and agentic workflow orchestration.
  • Build automated pipelines using tools like n8n, OpenClaw, and custom Python/JS scripts.
  • Integrate cutting-edge AI APIs directly into our production operational systems.
  • Assist in scaling our internal intelligence and efficiency infrastructure.

Graphic Designer

Design Part Time

Help us shape the visual identity of Doable Group and our evolving apparel sub-brands. You will create assets spanning digital marketing, social media campaigns, and product packaging.

Requirements & Responsibilities:
  • Design compelling marketing collateral, apparel graphics, and social media assets.
  • Ensure rigorous brand consistency across all physical and digital touchpoints.

UX/UI Designer

Design Full Time

E-commerce Operations Manager

Operations Full Time

Oversee the day-to-day digital operations of our fashion sub-brands. You will manage drop campaigns, synchronize inventory data, and maintain our CRM infrastructure.

Requirements & Responsibilities:
  • Coordinate inventory management and fulfillment logistics for physical apparel drops.
  • Manage SLA tracking and customer data pipelines within our CRM (HubSpot).
  • Experience with headless commerce systems and e-commerce platforms (e.g., Shopify, Medusa).
  • Strong organizational skills to maintain daily operational workflows.
